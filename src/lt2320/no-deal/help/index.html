Help with Our Site

Problems? Confused! Help is here!

Home

The home page shows today's big deal and other available deals in your market.

If your browser supports drag-and-drop, simply drag an available deal to the "Your Deals" area to purchase it. Note that you will not be charged for the purchase (and unable to redeem it) until you have joined and provided your billing information.

Purchases are not refundable.

Join

Signup to purchase deals on the join page.

You must provide accurate and valid information for all fields. Once you have joined, you will be able to purchase and redeem deals.

Chirps

Find out what people are saying about DealingTree on the chirps page.

If your browser supports third-party Ajax requests, this page will show you the latest messages about DealingTree on the chirpinator.com microblogging site.

Savings

The savings page displays a chart of your savings.

If your browser supports Canvas drawing, and you have purchased at least one deal, the chart will include a line depicting your total savings with each new purchase. For each deal, a two-color bar depicts the value of the deal compared to the price you paid.

Demo

Watch a demonstration video on the demo page.

Your browser must support one of the available video formats or allow you to download the video to your computer.
