Environmental awareness has never been a better bargain! Accessorize your home or office with decor and other great products made from 100% recycled materials. All of our items begin as other products, which our collection crews gather from demolitions, commercial dumpsters and curbside trash piles. These perfectly- good discards are then shipped to our factories where they are melted down (all toxins are carefully expelled into the deep ocean or the ozone layer, as appropriate, so as not to degrade the air and water quality in the vicinity of our exectutive offices), poured into molds, hand- carved, hand- painted, hand- packaged and hand- delivered to our warehouse, where they are stockpiled until purchased or they go out of style. In the latter case, they begin the process anew; in fact, nearly eighty percent of our current merchandise has been through our reclamation process at least two times (or sometimes as many as a dozen times) before. So buy now, knowing that with every purchase, you might be helping the environment even more!
